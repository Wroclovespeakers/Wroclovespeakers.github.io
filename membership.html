Payment details

Membership fee gives you the possibility to fully benefit the meetings, i.e. to deliver speeches and evaluations and amounts to 195 zł for half a year. It is paid every April and October, or if someone starts between those months, their first fee is pro-rated accordingly (rounding up to 1 zł). The table below shows the pro-rated fees for each starting month.

Additionally, a new person that signs up for Toastmasters for the first time pays one-time registration fee of 95 zł for which they receive access to choose one Path in Pathways educational program and Toastmasters newspaper subscription.
